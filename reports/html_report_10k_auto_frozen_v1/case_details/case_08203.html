Many colorful plastic bowls are arranged in a circular pattern on a flat white surface, forming a radial design with larger bowls at the center and smaller ones radiating outward. The bowls come in various hues including red, yellow, green, black, pink, and orange, with some showing glossy finishes and others appearing matte. Several bowls have small metal fasteners or pins visible along their rims, suggesting they are mounted or attached to the wall. The arrangement creates a visually dynamic composition, with colors blending into each other as the size of the bowls decreases toward the edges. In the bottom right corner, the text “cvpts” is printed in small, lowercase letters. The lighting is even and bright, highlighting the smooth curves and reflective surfaces of the bowls without casting strong shadows. The overall display looks like a decorative art installation or wall sculpture.
