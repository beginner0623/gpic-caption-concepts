A large, rough-textured stone structure with a rounded top and a rectangular opening frames a view of the ocean. The stone is beige with visible cracks and uneven surfaces, and it has a dark, flat metal plate inside the opening. Through the gap, the blue water stretches to the horizon under a clear sky. Behind the stone, the sea appears calm with gentle waves, and the sky is light blue with no clouds. To the left of the stone’s base, a small patch of sand is visible, and the edge of the structure casts a shadow on the ground. The stone’s design includes two vertical cutouts on either side of the central opening, creating a narrow frame for the distant view. No text or markings are visible on the stone or in the background.
