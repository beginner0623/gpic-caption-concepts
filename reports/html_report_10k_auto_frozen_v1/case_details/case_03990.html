A vintage twin-lens reflex camera with a black body and silver metal components is positioned on a plain white surface. The camera’s design includes textured grip areas and visible screws, with a strap attachment point on the left side. The entire object is photographed from a slightly elevated angle, showing its top, right side, and front face clearly against the uncluttered background.
