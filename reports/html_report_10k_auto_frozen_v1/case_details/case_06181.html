The room contains numerous abstract sculptures made of stone, wood, and metal, arranged on the floor and on pedestals. Some pieces are tall and slender with layered vertical forms, while others are rounded or shaped like organic fragments with rough textures. A dark wooden chair sits in the foreground, next to a round stone base holding a small brown box and a golden orb. Behind the sculptures, there’s a white wall with a skylight above it, and a doorway leading to another space. To the right, a large carved wooden archway stands beside a white pedestal holding a curved sculpture. The floor is smooth and light gray, and the overall lighting is even and bright. Several smaller objects, including bowls and figurines, are scattered across the room’s surfaces.
