A circular, bright red object is centered within a metal ring, surrounded by numerous dark brown wooden slats that radiate outward in straight lines from the center. The wood appears aged and weathered, with visible grain and some darker staining along the edges. The red object has a slightly textured surface and is encircled by a thin, shiny metallic border. The wooden slats extend from the center point in all directions, creating a radial pattern that fills the entire frame. In the background, faint greenish-blue discoloration can be seen on some of the wood near the outer edges. The lighting highlights the contrast between the warm red center and the cool, shadowed tones of the surrounding wood. The composition suggests a view looking upward into a dome-like structure or ceiling made of these wooden beams.
