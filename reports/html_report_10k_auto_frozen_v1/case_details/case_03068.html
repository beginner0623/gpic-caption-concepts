A large, ornate stone building facade features a central arched doorway with intricate carvings on all sides. The ground in front of the building is paved with flat stones, and shadows from the architecture stretch across the pavement. The overall design suggests a historic religious or civic building with elaborate Baroque-style ornamentation.
