A large, ornate fireplace with a black stone surround sits centered against dark wooden paneling. Above the fireplace is an elaborate decorative mantelpiece painted in gold, blue, and red, featuring carved figures and a central shield emblem. The mantel has two tall columns with gold leaf patterns and small red shields on their sides. Inside the fireplace, a metal grate with intricate designs rests over a dark, empty hearth. The floor beneath is made of worn wooden planks with visible grain and color variation. To the right and left of the fireplace are dark wood doors or walls, and above the mantel is a plain white ceiling. Small red shields with a white cross are affixed to the lower part of each column.
