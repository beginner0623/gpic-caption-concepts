The knight is standing upright, wearing armor with visible shoulder plates and gauntlets, holding a long weapon that resembles a spear or halberd slanted across their body. The painting has a bold, graphic style with thick black outlines and flat areas of white and black, contrasting sharply with the vivid red field. The frame is dark and rectangular, mounted on a light-colored wall, and a small signature is visible in the bottom right corner of the canvas. The artwork is displayed indoors, with the edge of the wall and a faint shadow cast by the frame visible to the right. The composition is centered, with the knight facing slightly toward the viewer’s left, and the entire piece appears to be hung for exhibition.
