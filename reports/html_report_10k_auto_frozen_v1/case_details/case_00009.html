The figure appears to be in motion, with arms extended outward, while the creature has prominent horns and a textured body. The carving is set into a larger stone structure with rough, uneven edges visible around the relief. In the upper portion, a curved stone archway or border frames the top edge of the carving. The stone shows signs of aging, with some areas worn smoother than others, and the entire piece is lit by natural daylight. The texture of the stone is coarse and grainy, with shadows deepening the contours of the figures.
